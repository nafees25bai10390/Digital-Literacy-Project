Phishing Prevention Checklist

General Safety Tips
- Never share OTP, passwords, or bank details with anyone
- Always verify the sender before responding to emails or messages
- Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links
- Use strong, unique passwords for different accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Keep your device and apps updated regularly
- Install apps only from trusted sources like official app stores
- Log out from accounts when using public or shared devices

Email & Phishing Awareness
- Check the sender’s email address carefully (look for small spelling errors)
- Do not trust emails that create urgency (e.g., “Your account will be blocked immediately”)
- Avoid clicking on links in emails; instead, visit official websites directly
- Look for grammatical errors or unusual formatting in messages
- Never download attachments from unknown or suspicious emails
- Verify suspicious emails by contacting the organization directly

Website Safety
- Always check for “https://” and a secure lock icon before entering details
- Avoid entering personal information on unfamiliar websites
- Double-check website URLs for spelling mistakes (fake domains)

Financial & UPI Safety
- Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money
- Always verify payment requests before approving
- Do not share banking credentials over calls or messages
- Regularly monitor your bank transactions for any suspicious activity

Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
